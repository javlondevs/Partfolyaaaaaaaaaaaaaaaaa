About The Team

Power-X Robotics

We are Power-X, a top-tier robotics team founded in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. Our team was formed within a school environment and consists of carefully selected students, each chosen for their exceptional skills, discipline, and strong technical background.

Every member specializes in a specific field and contributes with confidence and responsibility, allowing our team to operate with precision and efficiency. What sets Power-X apart is our commitment to excellence, teamwork, and innovation.

We continuously strive to raise the standard of competitive robotics in Uzbekistan, approaching every challenge with determination, creativity, and respect for one another.
